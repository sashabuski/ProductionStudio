ABOUT

CCX Productions is an independent production company creating bold visuals and unforgettable stories. We mix creativity, technical skill, and a bit of chaos to bring ideas to life in a way that feels fresh and real.

Clients work with us because we care about the work as much as they do. Every project gets full attention. Every detail matters. Whether it is film, music videos, commercials, or experimental pieces, we aim to make something that stands out and stays in people’s minds.
